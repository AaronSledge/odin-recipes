Chicken soup

If you are in the mood for a warm meal on a cold day, chicken noodle soup is the way to go. Chicken noodle soup originated from china, hundreds of years before the popular canned dish. If you follow this recipe you will able to make this soup within 30 minutes.

  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Broth(chicken and vegetable)
  • Chicken
  • Noodles
  • Carrots
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • butter
  • salt and pepper

  2. Add oniona and celery and cook for 5 minutes
