Gumbo

When you feel the need to have good old fashion southern food, you need to try to gumbo. The dish originated from Louisiana United States. It is a type of stew that usually contains sausage, chicken, or seafood with some type of onions and celery. This dish will in total will take about 2 to 3 hours to make.

  • all purpose flour
  • bacon drippings
  • chopped celery
  • large onion
  • large green pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Andouille sliced sausage
  • 3 quarts of water
  • beef bouillon
  • white sugar
  • salt
  • hot pepper sauce
  • cajun seasoning blend
  • bay leaves
  • dried thyme leaves
  • canned stewed tomatoes
  • tomato sauce
  • teaspoon file powder
  • frozen cut okra
  • distilled white vinegar
  • crabmeat
  • uncooked medium shrimp
  • worcestershire sauce
  1. Whisk flour and 3/4 cup bacon dripping in large saucepan on medium heat untill smooth
  2. Whisk constantly untill mahogany color(20-30 minutes)
  3. Place celery, onion, green bell pepper, and garlic into food processor untill all are chopped
  4. Stir vegatables into the saucepan and mix in sausage
  5. Simmer mixture untill vegetables are tneder for 10 to 15 minutes
  6. Remove heat and put aside
  7. Combine water and beef bouillon cube in a large soup pot and boil over medium to high heat
  8. Stir untill bouillon cubes dissolve and add the roux into the boiling water
  9. Reduce heat to simmer and mix sugar, salt, hot pepper sauce, Cajun seasoning, bay leaves, thyme, stewed tomatoes, and tomato sauce
  10. Simmer soup over low heat for about 1 hour
  11. Mix 2 teaspoons of file gumbo powder around the 45 minute mark
  12. Melt 2 tablesppons of bacon drippings in a skillet over medium heat
  13. Add okra and vinegar and cook for 15 minutes
  14. Remove okra and stir into simmering gumbo
  15. Mix in reserved vegetab;es. crabmeat, shrimp, and worestershire sauce and simre for 45 minutes
